About Diego

Diego Stevens - Burke is a freelance UX/UI designer. He is a passionate, dedicated, hard working individual. He enjoys solving user problems to make humans satisfied when using digital products. Collaboration and redesign are of great interest to him. He is always ready and open, challenging myself and trying new things.

With a background in engineering, Diego is very experienced in understanding the root problems and finding efficient solutions to them. As he as learned more about UX and human-centered design, he knew that he wanted to be a part of the actively growing solutions that would make a purposeful impact on users.

After completing some previous coding courses and dabbling with graphic design platforms before Diego wanted to find a balance of the two, so in 2020 Diego transitioned into a career of UX design. He feels UX design is a great intersection of creative and analysis work. There is so much room for creative expression but user experience design focuses on the user and solutions to real human needs.

Diego has studied UX design through the immersive instruction and complex projects based program with University of Toronto continuing studies UX/UI Bootcamp. The program has provided Diego with the whole UX design process and fundamentals of front-end web development. Through the courses he has grown a great interest in the UX portion of the process. He is also going to pursue future education in UI design.
